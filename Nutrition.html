A bodyweight workout is a type of strength training exercise that uses an individuals own weight to provide resistance against gravity1. These exercises can enhance a range of biomotor abilities, including strength, power, endurance, speed, flexibility, coordination, and balance1. Common bodyweight exercises include push-ups, pull-ups, squats, and sit-ups1. Bodyweight workouts are convenient because they generally do not require any equipment and can be performed anywhere, making them ideal for those who travel or do not have access to a gym1.
